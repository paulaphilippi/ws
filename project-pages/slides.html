2025 - Society for Ambulatory Assessment Conference

In May 2025, I held a talk at the Society for Ambulatory Assessment Conference in Leuven on influencing factors of short-term stress reactions in the daily lives of youths based on preliminary results of the READY-study; especially comparing effects of different baseline modeling variants.

2023 - 32nd Rehabilitation Colloquium

In February 2023, I held a talk at the 32nd Rehabilitation Colloquium in Hanover on opportunities and drivers of digital health interventions in the rehabilitation context and the acceptance of care providers and recipients towards them. Specifically, I presented findings from our model validation and further development of the unified theory of acceptance and use of technology (Philippi et al., 2021).
